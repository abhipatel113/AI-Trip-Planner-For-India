
AI Trip planner for India.

## Features

- City / days / budget inputs, plus **travel style** and **dietary preference** selectors
- Day-by-day itinerary with morning / afternoon / evening + food picks
- **Inline editing** of every day before finalizing
- **Finalize & save** the itinerary to an in-session history (sidebar)
- **View / delete / download JSON** of any saved trip
- Friendly loading spinner and categorized error messages (rate limit, quota, network, invalid response)
